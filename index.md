# Redline Empire Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

## Overview
Redline Empire ("the game") is developed by Chris Berg. 
This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection
Redline Empire does not collect or store any personal data 
on our servers. Game progress is saved locally on your 
device only.

## Analytics
We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous 
gameplay data such as session length, features used, and 
game progress. This data cannot be used to identify you 
personally and helps us improve the game.

## Crash Reporting
We use Google Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous 
crash reports. This helps us identify and fix bugs. No 
personal information is collected.

## Advertising
Ads are displayed via Google AdMob. AdMob may use your 
device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on 
Android) to show relevant ads. You can opt out of 
personalized ads in your device settings:
- iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking
- Android: Settings → Google → Ads

## In-App Purchases
In-app purchases are processed by:
- Apple App Store (iOS)
- Google Play Store (Android)

We do not store your payment information. All transactions 
are handled securely by Apple and Google.

## Data Retention
Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing 
to retain or delete.

## Children's Privacy
Redline Empire is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. 
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Third Party Services
- Google Firebase Analytics
- Google Firebase Crashlytics  
- Google AdMob
- RevenueCat (purchase validation)
- Apple App Store
- Google Play Store
